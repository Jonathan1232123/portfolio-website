Blender Project

This Blender project showcases a realistic 3D model of a couch. I carefully crafted the overall shape by manipulating and combining multiple geometric forms. To enhance authenticity, I applied detailed 3D textures that complement the aesthetic and feel of the design. Finally, I set up a backdrop with a classic three-point lighting system to highlight the model’s features and recorded a short rotating video to showcase the final presentation.

This group project focused on designing a food delivery mobile application from the ground up. Working collaboratively, we went through the full design process — starting with initial mockups, refining them into high-fidelity designs, and developing an interactive prototype using Figma. We also created a detailed user flow to map out the entire navigation of the app, ensuring a smooth and intuitive user experience. To validate our design decisions, we conducted A/B testing and gathered feedback to refine usability and visual consistency. The final product highlights a modern interface, efficient navigation, and an engaging overall experience for users ordering and tracking their meals.
